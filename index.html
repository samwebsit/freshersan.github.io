The students of 2nd & 3rd year from Dept. of Computer Science organised Fresher's party on Aug 27, 2022 to welcome the freshly inducted batch of 2022. Sangameshwar College organize the fresher's party every year.
The purpose of Fresher's Party is to welcome new students in a friendly atmosphere and to encourage their creative impulses to boost their confidence. It is the day where seniors and juniors finally bond and unite to celebrate being part of the college. The senior batch students welcome their juniors, all guest and teachers. The program starts with 'Aarti'. And it is accomplished by so many exciting events and programs like games, traditional, fusion, and modern dances, breathtaking singing performances, and splendid decoration, thus making it a soulful experience.
The sangameshwar College Solapur explored the talent of the beginners and existing students through this fresher's party. Moreover, these types of celebrations help juniors to form bonds and ensure good coordination with seniors. This leads to a better understanding among all..!
